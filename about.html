About Us

What We Provide

Networking

Network with alumni and current students who are willing to help guide you along your path towards success. We have a vast network of consultants, researchers, investment bankers, engineers, entrepreneurs, and much more.

Consulting Projects

Develop your analytical skills through case studies. You will get the opportunity to work with local companies in order to find solutions that will help them improve their business.

Mentorship

Experience mentorship as members of our club will seek to help you and guide you in your professional career. The problems you're having right now are problems that all of us have had at some point and we can help you.

Company Tours

Travel and tour local companies to get an inside look of professional life after college.

Professional Workshops

Become the best you can be. With professional workshops, we will continually push each other to improve our networking, resumes, interview skills, etc.

Career Development

Not sure about your career? We can help. With an active body of over 40 students, all of us are here to help push you to become the best you can be.

Undergraduate Business Network is an organization dedicated to serving the UCSD undergraduate community. We aim to expand the community's knowledge of various aspects of the business world through activities such as resume critiquing, interview preparation, mentorship, networking sessions, and much, much more. Our goal is to provide as many opportunities for UCSD undergraduate students as possible to discover what it means to be a true professional, and to prepare students for the life after college, whether that be in a graduate school, a start-up, or a Fortune 500 company. Anything can be a business, and our organization tries to emulate that thinking through the diverse majors of all our members, from Economics to Engineering to the Arts.

Our Mission

Message From The President

On behalf of the Undergraduate Business Network, I would like to extend to you a warm welcome to our website. UBN is a pre-professional organization founded in 2014 whose mission is to harness passion and nurture business in our university. As an organization, we are more than just group of students; we are a family. We not only work to succeed individually, but strive to see each and every member succeed as well.

As part of the Undergraduate Business Network, we are united by our passion for business, our ambition to succeed, and our aspiration to lead. Taking advantage of our expansive number of resources, professional and academic guidance and support, and extensive network, we seek to develop successful business leaders and visionaries.

We welcome you to explore our website and learn more about the members of the Undergraduate Business Network at the University of California, San Diego. UBN encourages you to join our organization to discover the different experiences and opportunities that we have to offer. We invite you to surround yourself with aspiring professionals, and we challenge you to be the best you can be.

Sincerely,

Daniel Wen | UBN President
