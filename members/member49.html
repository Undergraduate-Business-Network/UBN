  • Biography: Daniel is a senior pursuing a degree in Bioengineering: Bioinformatics with a minor in Business. He was born in LA, grew up in Brazil, and went to high school in Virginia. This past year he has been working at Bionomics, a biopharmaceutical company based in Australia. His company and he are currently working on a monoclonal antibody that targets cancer stem cells, and this past summer it was approved as an IND for cancer treatment. Daniel is also interested in business and leadership by serving as the president of UBN for 2 years and by being part of the TEDxUCSD team. In the future, he hopes to be pursue a career in the biotech industry and start his own company or license a drug. After graduation, he plans to work in the biotech industry for a few years and then pursue a PhD or an MBA. Daniel enjoys to cook, play tennis, travel, and read non-fiction books in his spare time.
