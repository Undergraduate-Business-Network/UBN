Youli Wang

  • Major: Joint Mathematics-Economics
  • Year: Sophomore

  • Positions Held: Marketing Committee
  • Biography: Youli is a sophomore currently pursuing her bachelor degree in Mathematics and Economics. She is the Director of Marketing in Thurgood Marshall College Honor’s Council and is working on the welcome event for the incoming freshman. Youli is interested in Marketing and investment banking. Despite being fully involved on campus, she also prepares herself in her career. She did two researches about Marketing in 2014. The first research is about how the pricing strategy that Kentucky Fried Chicken (KFC) used in China increased their annual revenue in 2013 by 12% than the last year. The other one was about the role of color while considering the attraction of food packaging to children. She won the National Scholarship in Zhejiang University because of these two research projects. She was also one of the RA in Zhejiang university’s Ph.D program. Youli enjoys playing guitar and jogging in her spare time.
